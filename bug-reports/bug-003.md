# Bug: Double-clicking submit creates duplicate records

## Severity
High

## Justification
Duplicate employee or overtime records can affect payroll calculations.

## Steps to Reproduce
1. Open the employee creation form.
2. Enter valid employee details.
3. Double-click the Submit button.

## Expected Result
Only one employee record should be created.

## Actual Result
Multiple records are created.

## Impact
Duplicate records may lead to duplicate payroll processing and incorrect salary payments.

## Root Cause
The submit button is not disabled after the first click.
